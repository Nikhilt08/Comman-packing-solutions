Our Products

We manufacture premium-quality edge protection solutions designed for safe storage, palletizing, and transportation of goods.

Angle Board

Our Angle Boards provide superior edge and corner protection during storage and transit. Manufactured using high-strength paperboard materials, they protect goods from impact, vibration, and load shifting.

Suitable for furniture, appliances, industrial goods, and export packaging applications.

Notched Angle Board

Notched Angle Boards are designed with precision slots that allow easy bending and wrapping around products.

They provide enhanced stability and flexibility, ensuring goods remain protected during handling and transportation.

Water-Resistant Angle Board

Our Water-Resistant Angle Boards are specially designed for environments exposed to moisture and humidity.

They offer excellent durability and structural strength, making them ideal for export and long-distance shipping.

Self Adhesive Angle Board

Self Adhesive Angle Boards are laminated with adhesive for secure and easy application.

They provide stable stacking, palletizing support, and reliable edge protection for industrial goods.

Printed & Colored Angle Board

Printed and Colored Angle Boards combine product protection with branding opportunities.

Custom logos, brand names, and graphics can be printed directly on the surface, turning packaging into a marketing tool.
